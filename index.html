Markerless Human Pose Estimation for Sports

From ordinary video to 2D/3D kinematics. Learn pipelines, core algorithms, deployment trade-offs, and validation against motion capture.

Why markerless? Labs with markers are precise but expensive and intrusive. Modern CV quantifies technique, workload, and injury risk proxies from commodity cameras. You’ll learn sensors, kinematics, validation, and deployment.

Learning Objectives

  • Design a single/multi‑view capture setup, with depth & IMU options.
  • Compute 2D/3D joint angles, velocities, symmetry indices.
  • Diagnose failure modes and mitigate them.
  • Deploy real‑time on edge devices.
  • Validate against gold‑standard motion capture.
