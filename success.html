Successes and Failures

Where it shines

  • Controlled training footage (stable camera, single athlete).
  • On‑device feedback for drills (sprint starts, squats).
  • Sport‑specific priors help instantaneous actions (e.g., pitching).

Failure modes & Mitigations

  • Occlusion/crowding: elevated camera; widen FOV; bottom‑up models.
  • Rapid rotations: IMU fusion; faster shutter; multi‑view.
  • Blur/lighting: fast shutter + lighting; avoid auto‑exposure hunting.
  • Sync drift: clap/LED resync; log NTP offsets.
