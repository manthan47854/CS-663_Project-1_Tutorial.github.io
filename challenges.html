The Challenges

  • Generalization: domain shift across athletes/sports. Use synthetic data, domain randomization, adaptation, uncertainty.
  • Temporal reasoning: transformers/GNNs without over‑smoothing; try local‑global attention, dilated windows.
  • Real‑time: INT8 quantization, operator fusion, batch=1, stride tuning, delegates.
  • Validity: MPJPE, angle RMSE, cycle timing error, Bland–Altman bias/limits.
