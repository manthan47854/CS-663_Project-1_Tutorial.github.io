Sensor Possibilities

Start with a single RGB camera at 30–60 fps. For 3D stability and depth disambiguation, add multi‑view cameras or a depth camera. Optional IMU fusion reduces drift and resolves occlusions.

Camera Placement

  • Orthogonal views: sagittal + frontal for running/cutting.
  • Height: hip‑to‑chest level; minimize perspective distortion.
  • Shutter: ≥1/500s outdoors; add light; raise ISO as needed.
  • Lens: 24–35mm equiv to reduce rolling‑shutter and distortion.

Multi‑View Calibration & Sync

  1. Estimate intrinsics (fx,fy,cx,k1..) via checkerboard.
  2. Solve extrinsics (R,t) in the action volume.
  3. Synchronize via hardware trigger or NTP + clap/LED.
  4. Verify reprojection error < 0.8 px.

Depth & IMUs

  • Depth: robust indoors; ToF beats structured‑light in sun.
  • IMUs: complementary/UKF fusers stabilize orientation.
  • Mounting: distal segments (feet, wrists) capture velocity well.
Tip: Record a 5–10s clip per drill for repeatable benchmarking.
